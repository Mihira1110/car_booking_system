About City Taxi (PVT) Ltd

Founded in 2024, City Taxi (PVT) Ltd is a leading provider of transportation solutions in the city. We pride ourselves on offering reliable, safe, and affordable services to both passengers and drivers. With a modern fleet of vehicles, a dedicated team of professionals, and cutting-edge technology, City Taxi is committed to making every trip comfortable and efficient.

Our Mission

At City Taxi, our mission is to transform the urban transportation experience by providing an outstanding service that prioritises safety, convenience, and customer satisfaction. We aim to bridge the gap between passengers and drivers through advanced technology and exceptional customer support.

Our Services

  • Passenger Transport: Book a taxi with ease using our web-based portal or mobile app, with real-time tracking and notifications.
  • Driver Opportunities: Become a City Taxi driver and enjoy flexible working hours, competitive earnings, and robust support from our team.
  • Corporate Services: Special packages for businesses and organisations, providing efficient and professional transport for employees.
  • 24/7 Availability: We offer round-the-clock service to ensure that our passengers are always able to get a ride, no matter the time or location.

Our Technology

We leverage the latest technologies to offer seamless booking, real-time location tracking, SMS notifications, and a user-friendly web interface. Our platform is designed to ensure fast response times and efficient resource management for both passengers and drivers.
